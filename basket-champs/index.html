Basket Champs
RavalMatic

About

Basket Champs is a really fun basketball tournament game, where you shoot, win, advance in the competetions, and win the tournament! Select your team and move your mouse around to adjust your shooting angle, height, and distance, to make sure your ball goes in! The basket can move up, down, left, and right, so measure your distance and time your shot perfectly to score! Whoever scores the most points wins the game. Avoid being eliminated in the first 5 games in the group phase to move on to the tournament phase! Win all the games and bring the victory home!

Controls

  • Mouse: Aim
  • Click: Shoot

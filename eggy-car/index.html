Eggy Car
Beedo Games

About

In this fun and highly skilled car driving game, you have only one goal. Don't let the egg drop to the ground! Manuever the car around the hills and valleys cleverly to keep the egg in your car's container and try not to let it drop and crack! Stop or go forward at just the right time to keep your egg balanced. Collect coins on your ride to buy new cars and try out new strategies! Have fun!

Controls

  • Left Arrow / A / Left Pedal: Go Back
  • Right Arrow / D / Right Pedal: Go Forward
